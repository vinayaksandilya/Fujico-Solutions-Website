Air Conditioning Service

There is a strong need to get every electrical device serviced at regular intervals of time and so does your air conditioner. Every Air Conditioner requires timely servicing so that it can work properly. At Fujico heating & cooling solutions, we are known for offering world-class AC servicing to prevent it from any damage and ensure that it works properly all through the season. Most of the air conditioners required servicing during the start of summer while others should be serviced at the end. This regular servicing improves the system resilience and increase the longevity of your cooling system.

Your air conditioner is a machine that has a number of coils, filters and fans that require regular cleaning and maintenance so that your system can work properly without any glitch. If you do not get your AC maintained and serviced on time then problems such as excessive heating, sudden break-down or even short circuit. Apart from that, it may also consume a lot of energy as compared to the use. We, at Fujico heating & cooling solutions, give a thorough check to your electronic device and find out what the problem actually is.

Our technicians are experienced in offering the best service to your air conditioner. They ensure that the refrigerant is in the desired amount; there are no leakages and whether the thermostat is accurate or not apart from regular testing.
